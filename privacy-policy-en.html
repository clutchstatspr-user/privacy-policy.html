Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 22, 2025

1. Information We Collect

ClutchStats collects the following information to provide and improve our services:

  • Account Information: Name, last name, email address, and username
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the application, including tournaments, teams, players, and statistics
  • Device Information: Device ID, device model, and operating system
  • Subscription Information: Subscription status, start and expiration dates

2. How We Use the Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Authenticate users and manage subscriptions
  • Synchronize data across devices
  • Send service-related notifications
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely:

  • Locally: On your device using encryption
  • In the Cloud: On Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) with industry-standard security measures
  • Encryption: Sensitive data such as passwords and subscription dates are encrypted

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Google Play: To process subscription payments
  • Firebase (Google): To store and synchronize data
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law

5. Security

We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure authentication through Firebase Authentication
  • Security rules in Firestore to protect your data
  • Offline validation with locally encrypted data

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data
  • Cancel your subscription at any time

7. Cookies and Similar Technologies

ClutchStats does not use cookies or tracking technologies. We use Firebase Analytics anonymously to improve our services.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

9. Children

ClutchStats is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
